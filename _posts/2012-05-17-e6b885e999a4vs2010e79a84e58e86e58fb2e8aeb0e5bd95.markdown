把如下粘贴到文本文件里，另存为批处理文件。（后缀为 *.bat）双击执行就可。  

    
    @echo off
    cd 
    @echo on
    @REG Delete HKEY_CURRENT_USERSoftwareMicrosoftVisualStudio10.0FileMRUList /va /f
    @REG Delete HKEY_CURRENT_USERSoftwareMicrosoftVisualStudio10.0ProjectMRUList /va /f
    @echo off
    cmd
    @echo on
